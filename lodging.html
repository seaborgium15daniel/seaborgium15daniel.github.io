Where to Stay

The Rock Harbor Lodge

About the Rock Harbor Lodge

Rock Harbor Lodge provides visitors accommodations in this unique National Park. Located along the shore of Lake Superior, Rock Harbor Lodge has rooms with private baths, cottages, and services include a dining room, snack bar, gift shop, dockside store, marina, rental motor boats, kayaks and canoes, guided fishing and sightseeing tours. Rates are based on double occupancy. Rollaway beds may be added to accommodate a maximum of six people.

A deposit equal to the first night's lodging guarantees your reservation. 90% of the deposit will be refunded if the reservation is cancelled at least 5 days prior to arrival. Gratuities are not included in the rates. Check-out time is 10:30am EDT.

Rates do not include state taxes, and are subject to approval by the National Park Service, Department of the Interior, and may change without notice.

Reservations can be made by calling:

  • Summer: 906-337-4993
  • Winter: 866-644-2003

Lakeside Lodge Rooms

Lakeside lodge rooms provide a spectacular view of Lake Superior. Rooms can accommodate 4 people.

Also, lodgers are allowed to dine at the Rock Harbor Lighthouse Restaraunt.

During the peak season(summer, from late-July to mid-August), the rates are $256/night, $60 extra per additional adult. During the non-peak season, rates are $231/night, $53 extra per additional adult. A half-day use of canoe is included.
